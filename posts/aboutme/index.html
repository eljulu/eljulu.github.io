I am a passionate foodie and photographer.

Two of my favourite things are food and travel. Trying out new restaurants is always an exciting experience to me. Exploring different kinds of food is a fascinating and intimate way to understand the culture as well as life of people. Besides that, I also enjoy cooking by myself. I was born in Hunan province in China. Being abroad, I miss hometown cuisine all the time. Cooking is my way to go back to my hometown and reminisce my childhood memories.
