Controllable rotation of electromagnetically levitated object

Aug 5 2019

Project Description [Report] [Video]

The goal of the project is to achieve controlled rotation of electromagnetically suspended objects. We are required to include a rotary control system in the suspension control system, which is provided by our sponsor. Within a duration of 3 months, my team members and I successfully developed a stable system to achieve this rotary control on the suspended system. Suggestion on further improvements are provided to our sponsor as well.

My Roles

  • Active and strongly motivated member of a team of five
  • Generated the final solution concept and fully involved in design and manufacture
  • Fully responsible for the design and test of the PID rotation control system
  • Good interaction with team members, close follow-ups on project schedule and planning
