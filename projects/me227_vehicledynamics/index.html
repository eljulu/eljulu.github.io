Stanford Niki Vehicle Dynamics and Control

May 7 2020

Project Description [Report]

This project involves developing two types of vehicle controller for Stanford Niki on the steering angle as well as the throttle such that the vehicle can run two laps on a specified track automatically with small deviation (error) from the path as well as reasonable lateral and longitudinal acceleration for the comfort of the passengers as well as the physical probability of dynamic performance of the vehicle.

My role

  • Active member of a team of four
  • Analytically derived the PID controller model
  • PID controller coding as well as parameters adjustments

Results

  • Vehicle performance in simulation reached the standard of the project
